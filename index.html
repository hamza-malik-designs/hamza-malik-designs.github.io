── Common Questions ──

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

What services do you offer?

I offer a comprehensive range of digital services including social media marketing & management (content strategy, scheduling, community management, analytics), graphic design (social media posts, banners, carousels, campaign creatives), brand identity & logo design (logos, brand guidelines, stationery), video editing & content creation (Reels, Shorts, YouTube, motion graphics), and web design & UI/UX (responsive websites, landing pages, prototyping). Each service is tailored to your specific business goals.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on the scope, complexity, and duration of each project. I offer competitive rates for individual projects, monthly retainers, and package deals. Contact me for a free consultation and custom quote — I'll provide a transparent breakdown with no hidden fees. My goal is to deliver premium quality at fair prices.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes! I work remotely with clients worldwide, including businesses in Pakistan, UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, UK, USA, and beyond. I'm comfortable with different time zones and use tools like Zoom, Google Meet, WhatsApp, and Slack for seamless communication. Most of my current clients are international.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies by project type: Social media post designs take 24-48 hours, logo design takes 3-5 business days, full brand identity takes 1-2 weeks, and ongoing social media management is on a monthly basis. I also offer rush delivery for urgent projects. Every project is delivered on time — guaranteed.

What tools do you use for design?

I use professional-grade tools including Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator for advanced design work, Canva Pro for rapid graphic creation, Figma for UI/UX design, CapCut Pro for video editing, Meta Ads Manager for paid campaigns, and various project management tools like Trello and Google Sheets.

How do we get started?

Getting started is simple! Just send me an email at hamza.malik.designss@gmail.com, message me on WhatsApp at +92 312 5956779, or connect on LinkedIn. We'll discuss your project requirements, goals, timeline, and budget. Once we agree on the scope, I'll send a proposal and we can begin right away. The initial consultation is always free!
